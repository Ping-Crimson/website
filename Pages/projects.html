The Stock Market experiment →

This project was done as a part of an investment teaching program. We were given a certain amount of money and had to invest it into a simulation of the current market. I used a low risk investment strategy. At first I didn't do very well, but I slowly improved after regornizing my portfolio several times. I finished 6th place (out of nearly 50 people) with 10% in growth after a few weeks.
